Introduction

“The best way to acquire knowledge is to share it”

Silicon Institute of Technology (SIT) along with Orissa Information Technology Society (OITS) is organizing a 3days workshop on “Computer Security” to keep pace with rapidly changing global scenario of software industry.

Objective

• To augment the knowledge of teachers on the subject.

• To enable faculty for pursuing research and development activities in this field.

Topics to be covered

• Elementary Cryptography

• Database Security

• Operating System Security

• Network Security

Who can Participate

• Researcher Scholars / Students intending to work in the field of computer security.

About Silicon

Silicon marked its presence in the field of technical education with a difference from the year 1999. The campus is situated in a beautiful, landscaped green stretch of land at the footsteps of Silicon Hills in Patia, Bhubaneswar. Silicon facilitates a teaching learning process where education is revered and rewarded.

About OITS

Orissa Information Technology Society (OITS) was established in the year 1997 in the state of Orissa with the noble thought of bringing information technology to grass root level of the state. Since its inception it has proved its substance by organizing many workshops and other development programs in the field.
