OBJECTIVE

NCIT-97 aims at bringing academics, engineers and scientists together for close exchange of views on state-of-art technologies of multifarious and widely growing areas of computer applications. Not only it will serve as an important forum for participation of technical community, also it will cater to development of general awareness on Information Technology. The conference will have invited talks,contributed oral paper presentations, panel discussions.

TOPICS

Includes but not limited to:

1. Database Management Systems
2. Internet Computing
3. S/W Engineering
4. Artificial Intelligence
5. High-speed Computing

PANEL DISCUSSION

IT and its Perspective
Computer Literacy and Computer Education

CALL FOR PAPER

Authors are requested to submit Five copies of Papers double spaced written English, of up to 12 pages including figures,tables and references etc. The format should be A4 in a Roman Font,12 Font size. In addition, one sheet must be attached including :Titles of the paper,authors' Name, a list of five keywords,the corresponding author information(name,postal and email address,Phone&Fax number).

The interested authors are requested to submit extended abstracts to PROGRAM CHAIR with detail contact address. (If they prefer, they may send the abstracts to chitta@cs.utep.edu.) An abstract should clearly identify author's contributions and its length should not be more than 1500 words.
