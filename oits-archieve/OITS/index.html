ORISSA INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SOCIETY (oits)

 

OITS was formed in 1997 (and registered in 1998) to promote information technology in the state of Orissa in the east coast of India.

The aims and objectives:

  • To promote education, research and interaction between academic institutions and industry in Information Technology (IT)
  • To promote collaboration among Information Technology personnel in Orissa at National and International level
  • To promote usage of Information Technology in Orissa
  • To organise conference/symposia and workshops in IT areas
  • To pubish periodicals and journals in IT
  • To identify and encourage young talents in IT
  • To felicitate persons having outstanding contribution for education, research and technology
    • in IT
  • To raise funds for Society through membership subscription, donation, grants, loans, subsidies from local bodies, State and Central and other institutions, companies and international agencies and voluntary organisations or individuals, who are prepared to assist the Society for attainment of its objectives on a non-profit basis.
Membership:
Any professional in Information Technology or interested in the same shall be eligible to be a member of OITS.[ Download Membership Form ]
The following will be the categories of membership:
  • Life members(L) Rs.3000/-
  • Student members(S) Rs.500/-
  • Institutional members(I) Rs.10000/-
  • Donor members(D) Rs.5000/-
  • Patrons(P) Rs.25,000/-

Registered office:

147, Kanan Vihar HIG Colony, Patia, Bhubaneswar 751031.
The area of operation of the Society shall be whole of India. The Society can establish branch office(s) anywhere in the state of Orissa or outside and expand the activities of the Society.
For the purpose of this document, "Society" whenever and wherever mentioned hereafter will mean Orissa Information Technology Society (OITS).
