OCIT (OITS International Conference on Information Technology) is a premier international conference and forum for high quality research in the area of Information Technology. OCIT2025 will be held at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar (https://www.kiit.ac.in/) in collaboration with Orissa IT Society (http://www.oits-icit.org/).
